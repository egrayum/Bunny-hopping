Flagstaff research

Climbing

There are 2 main sport areas near Flagstaff: Elden Mountain and The Pit. Elden Mountain is just north of flagstaff, and has many scattered, smallish walls, each hosting somewhere from 3-15 routes. The grade range is mostly 5.10 to 5.12, with a couple 13’s and the occasional easier route. There is also lots of bouldering scattered around the area. The rock is dacite.

The climbing at The Pit is mostly steeper and short, and some walls are very huecoed. The style is pocketed and edgy, as the rock is white Kaibab limestone. It is a short drive southeast of Flagstaff. To quote mountain project: “Today, it’s northern Arizona’s prime sport area. Well, if by prime means most convenient for Flagstaff locals.” The grade range is 5.7 to 5.14, with most walls having a very high concentration one number grade from 10 to 13.

There is also the Overlook, between Flagstaff and Sedona, which is almost all trad.

Hiking/Biking

There are 80+ trails in or very near Flagstaff on All Trails, and around 50 of them are dog friendly and 50 are mountain bike friendly. There are also hundreds more trails within a 30 minute drive of Flagstaff.

Groceries

The grocery store options in Flagstaff are your average stores in a medium sized town, even if there is a decently sized selection: there’s a couple Safeways, a Walmart or two, a Whole Foods, Fry’s, Bashas, and Sprouts.

Housing/Lots

Houses in Flagstaff are listed at $451k on average, which is up +16.5% from last year. Price/sqft is $246 on average, up +11.8% from last year. They also tend to sell for -1% list price and usually go pending in 17 days. Lots can have pretty good deals (I think), there are several lots on Zillow that are 10+ acres for <$130k and have been listed for >100 days. They are several miles from Flagstaff. Other lots, < 2 acres, are much closer to Flagstaff and are much more expensive per acre.

Temps

The average high stays between 50˚F and 85˚F all year except for Dec, Jan, and Feb, during which the avg high is 44-47˚F. The highest avg. high of the year is ≈83˚F from Jun-Aug. The avg low stays around 30˚F below the avg high.

Travelling

It’s 1,250 miles to drive from Flagstaff to Portland, so for round trip it would be ≈$415 for gas (not towing the camper). There is an airport in Flagstaff that has flights to Portland, starting at an average of $240 round trip for 1 adult.
